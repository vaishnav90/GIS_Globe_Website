Our Team

Meet the dedicated professionals and alumni who drive innovation in GIS education and youth development. Our board of directors brings decades of combined experience in geospatial technology, education, and youth leadership.

From founding members who established our mission to current leaders shaping the future of GIS education, each team member contributes unique expertise and passion for empowering the next generation of geospatial innovators.

Our Board of Directors

Our board of directors consists of experienced leaders who have dedicated their careers to advancing GIS education and youth development. Each member brings unique expertise from government, academia, industry, and non-profit sectors.
